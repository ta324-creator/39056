Privacy Policy.

How we handle your information — plainly written, no legalese.

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Applies to lunarpainting.com

Who we are

Lunar Painting is a licensed painting contractor based in Laguna Hills, California, serving residential and commercial clients throughout Orange County and Los Angeles County. We operate this website at lunarpainting.com. You can reach us at lunarpainting1@gmail.com or (949) 592-7099.

Information we collect

We collect information you give us directly when you contact us or request an estimate. This includes:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Project address and description
  • Any other details you include in your message

We do not currently run analytics software, advertising pixels, or tracking scripts on this website. If that changes, this policy will be updated before any tracking goes live.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your estimate request or inquiry
  • Schedule and coordinate your painting project
  • Follow up after project completion if needed
  • Send invoices and project documentation

We do not use your information for marketing to unrelated businesses, and we do not add you to any mailing list without your consent.

Information sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone, ever. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use standard email and scheduling tools to run our business. These providers may process your data on our behalf and are bound to protect it.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Lunar Painting or others.

Cookies and third-party embeds

This website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. Our contact page embeds a Google Maps iframe which may set its own cookies subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We don't use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies won't prevent you from using this website.

Data retention

We keep project and contact records for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the project, comply with legal obligations (such as contractor licensing requirements in California), and resolve any disputes. If you would like your information deleted, contact us and we will do so to the extent we are not legally required to retain it.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. If you have concerns about submitting sensitive information, feel free to call us directly instead.

California residents — your rights under CCPA

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to know: You can request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to delete: You can request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to correct: You can request that we correct inaccurate personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to opt out of sale: We do not sell personal information. This right does not apply, but we acknowledge it.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at lunarpainting1@gmail.com or (949) 592-7099. We will respond within 45 days as required by California law.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Links to other websites

Our website may link to third-party sites such as Yelp, the BBB, HomeAdvisor, and Instagram. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their policies before sharing personal information with them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes we will update the date prominently. Continued use of the website after a policy change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
