Transport FAQ.

Answers to the questions we hear most. Don't see yours? Call us.

What size boats can you transport?
We transport boats from 16ft up to about 60ft on oversize permitted loads. Standard trailerable vessels (up to ~8.5ft wide, 14ft tall, 53ft long) move without permits. Larger loads require oversize permits which we arrange.
Do you transport boats across state lines?
Yes. As a registered FMCSA motor carrier (USDOT #4434746) we can haul between our three primary states and many others on a case-by-case basis. Call and describe your route.
Can you pick up directly from a marina?
Yes. We coordinate with marinas and dry stack facilities for pick-up and delivery. We handle all logistics — you don't need to be on-site.
Do you handle oversize or wide-load permits?
Yes. Boats over 8.5ft wide or 14ft tall require oversize permits. We handle permitting, route surveys, and escorts where required. You don't need to manage any of this.
How is transport priced?
Transport is quoted by route and boat size — distance, beam (width), height, and any permitting requirements factor in. Call or submit the form below and we'll get you a firm number within 24 hours.
What if I just want the boat gone, not transported?
That's our removal service. We handle full removal, disposal, and title paperwork. See our Boat Removal & Disposal page for details.
