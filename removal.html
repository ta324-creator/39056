Removal FAQ.

Most answers are here. If yours isn't, call us — we pick up.

Call (424) 410-3376 WhatsApp Us
What types of boats do you remove?
Powerboats, sailboats, pontoons, cabin cruisers, houseboats, commercial fishing vessels, and most other types up to around 80ft. Something unusual? Call us — we'll be straight about whether we're the right fit.
Can you remove a boat without a title?
Yes. Abandoned and title-less vessels are a common job for us. We handle the surety bond process, title research, and any paperwork needed to legally clear you of the vessel.
Can you remove a sunken or grounded boat?
Yes. We have the equipment for soft-bottom haul-outs, crane lifts, and compressed-air extractions. Difficult-access and in-water jobs are quoted individually, but yes — we can help.
How much does removal cost?
Standard removal starts at $50 per linear foot. A 24ft boat is around $1,200, a 32ft around $1,600, a 40ft around $2,000. In-water extractions and storm jobs are quoted individually. Your price is locked before we touch anything.
What happens to the boat after you take it?
Boats in sellable condition are resold or donated. Non-salvageable vessels are properly dismantled and disposed of at licensed facilities. We handle title surrender and all environmental compliance — nothing comes back to you.
Do you work with insurance companies?
Yes. We coordinate with adjusters on storm claims, provide all required documentation, and work within the approval timeline. We've done enough insurance jobs to know exactly what adjusters need.
How quickly can you schedule a removal?
Most jobs are scheduled within 3–7 days of accepting the quote. Emergency jobs — marina deadlines, insurance situations, storm recovery — move faster. We'll give you a specific date, not a vague window.
