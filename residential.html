What to Expect on Cost

Honest pricing.
No surprises.

Every home is different and we always walk the property before committing to a number. Here's a realistic range for typical OC residential projects using Sherwin Williams products.

Exterior
$2,500–$7,500

Typical single-family home. Includes pressure washing, surface prep, primer where needed, and two finish coats. Final price depends on size, number of stories, and surface condition.

Interior — Full Home
$2,000–$6,000

Walls, ceilings, and trim throughout. Varies by room count, ceiling height, and detail work on built-ins or cabinets. Single rooms typically start around $400–$800.

Our Process

5 steps.
Every single job.

We follow the same disciplined process whether painting one room or an entire house. This is what separates a lasting finish from one that needs redoing in two years.

01
Getting the space ready

We move furniture, cover everything with plastic sheeting, and protect your floors with drop cloths. Not a brush stroke before your home is fully protected.

02
Proper surface preparation

Every hole filled, every crack sealed, every stain primed, every surface sanded smooth. The prep work is where the quality lives.

03
The painting

Premium Sherwin Williams products deliver a uniform finish that looks sharp now and holds up for years. We don't cut corners on coats or coverage.

04
Thorough cleanup

Every day and at project completion — we return your furniture, vacuum and sweep, and remove every piece of material. Your home is left cleaner than we found it.

05
Final walk-through together

We walk every room with you. Anything that needs touching up, we handle on the spot. We don't pack up until you're satisfied.
