What I'd do for a painting company Form submits to email (so the lead is never lost). After submission: Save the lead. Show a success screen. Button says: "Send Photos on WhatsApp" This way: You capture every lead. You still push them into WhatsApp where most conversations actually happen. If they close the tab, you still have their information. For contractors (painters, roofers, landscapers, concrete, remodeling), that's usually the highest-converting setup.
