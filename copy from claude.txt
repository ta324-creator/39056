3. Case Studies Page — Beattus Cattus
Title: From Zero to Festival — Building Beattus Cattus from the Ground Up
The situation: A new skateshop opened at Playa Hermosa in Ensenada. The owner had vision and a community behind him, but no brand identity, no digital presence, and a major event on the horizon — the inaugural SK8 Fest Ensenada, tied to the grand opening of one of the best skateparks in Baja California and Baja California Sur.
Our role: Marketing Director
What we built: Logo design and full brand identity · Instagram page setup and visual system · Creative direction for ongoing Instagram content · Promotional flyers and banners · Event promo video (concept, script, recording, editing) · Instagram Reels · Facebook ad management · Live event production support
The event: SK8 Fest Ensenada took place September 30–October 1, 2023. The organizer expected 250–300 people. About 500 showed up. 100 competitive skaters came from Mexicali, Tijuana, Ensenada, Sonora, Baja California Sur, San Diego, and Los Angeles. The event was covered by El Vigía, Ensenada's main media outlet, both in the announcement and in the post-event recap. It was free, publicly organized, and tied to local government initiatives around youth sports and community health. SK8 Fest is now an annual event.
